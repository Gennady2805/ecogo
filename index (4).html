Introducing EcoGo

EcoGo is a compact electric delivery vehicle platform designed specifically for gig-economy drivers on platforms like DoorDash, UberEats, and Instacart. Our mission is to provide an affordable, eco-friendly, and efficient EV leasing solution for the growing gig workforce.
